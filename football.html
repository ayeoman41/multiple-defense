MULTIPLE DEFENSE

Philopsophy

The overall intention of this defense is simplify a players alignment and assignment which will allow each individual player to play without much thinking. The reduction of thinking is designed to allow for execution at a high level. This playbook should be thought of as a toolbox. There is never a need for every single tool on every job. Use the tools that are of need and save the other tools for another job.

Alignment

Alignment is one of the most important things to know as a defender. It helps knowing where to be on each formation so that a team cannot formation you to gain a numbers advantage. Each defensive position in the playbook will start out with explaining each defenders alignment.

Note: The alignment will be shown from a 1 deep safety alignment. The coach can choose if 2 deep safeties are of better use.

Playbook setup

The playbook will start out with many of the terms that will be needed to develop an understanding of the playbook. The order in which the information will be provided will start with:

  1. Defensive Line
  2. Linebackers
  3. Secondary

DEFENSIVE LINE

The defensive line will consist of 4 players. Two defensive ends: The Alpha and the End. Two defensive tackles: The Nose and the Tackle. The Alpha will always align into the boundary and the End will align to the field of every offensive formation. If the the football is in the middle of the field, the Alpha will default to the left of the offense. The Nose will align to the weak side of the formation and the Tackle will align to the strong side of the formation.

Note: The boundary is the short side of the field if the football is one the hash. What a coach designates as the strong or weak side of an offensive formation is up to the coach.

    Linebackers

    The Linebacker position will consist of 3 players. The names that can be used are up to the defensive coach. In this playbook they will be called Mike, Will, and Sam. In some defenses they will line up depending on the strength of the formation. In this playbook the Sam will go to the wide side of the field and the Will will go to the boundary. The Mike linebacker will always be in the middle of the Sam and Will.

    Secondary

    Note:The defensive secondary will be shown from a 1 high safety.

    Cover 3 (Green) will be the defensive coverage. The one high safety will take the middle 1/3 between the hashes. The two corners will take the outside 1/3 of the field. The "strong" safety will always align to the wide side of the field, while the Will linebacker will always go to the boundary.
